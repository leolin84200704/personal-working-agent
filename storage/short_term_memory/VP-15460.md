### 舊版架構 (EMR-Backend)
- Quartz scheduler, 15 分鐘一次 (cronjob-dev.yml)
- 掃描單位: EMR_SFTP_SOURCE (vendor) → SFTP_FOLDER_MAPPING (folder)
- Parse 後用 order_clients 做 NPI → clinic routing
- Concurrency: volatile boolean + synchronized + 20min timeout
- 關鍵檔案:
  - EmrOrderScheduler.java (Quartz 設定)
  - GetOrderFromSFTP.java (SFTP 下載)
  - ParseOrder.java (HL7 處理 + concurrency guard)
  - ParseHL7.java (HL7 parse + order_clients lookup)
  - SaveToOldDB.java (寫 EMR_TRACKING_DATA)
